Frequently Asked Questions

How does the affiliate program work?

Our affiliate program allows you to earn commissions by referring new members to our platform. You'll receive a unique referral link to share with your network. When someone signs up through your link and purchases a coupon code, you earn a commission.

How much can I earn as an affiliate?

Our standard commission rate is 30% of each coupon sale you generate. Top performers can earn additional bonuses and move up to higher commission tiers with even greater earning potential.

Why do I need to purchase a coupon code?

The coupon code serves as your membership access to our affiliate program. It helps maintain the quality of our network by ensuring all members are serious about building their affiliate business. The one-time fee also gives you access to our premium marketing resources and support.

How do I get paid?

Commissions are paid weekly via bank transfer or mobile money to your preferred payment method. You can request payment once your balance reaches the minimum payout threshold of ₦5,000.

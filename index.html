How It Works

Getting started with the IMO State Citizen Feedback System is simple and straightforward

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your basic information to become a verified citizen of Imo State.

2

Submit a Complaint

Report an issue by providing details, location, and optional photos of the problem.

3

Track Resolution

Monitor the status of your complaint and receive updates on the resolution progress.

4

Provide Feedback

Rate the resolution and provide additional feedback to help improve services.
